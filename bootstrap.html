Mobile Apps

On the AirSoccer mobile app, you can manage all aspects of your blimp soccer experience with ease and convenience. From booking your next game session to checking availability and selecting your preferred pitch location within the blimp, everything is just a tap away. You can also view upcoming matches, join tournaments, and connect with fellow players through in-app messaging and social features. Track your stats and performance over time, receive real-time game updates, and explore exclusive offers and promotions tailored specifically for our loyal AirSoccer community. Whether you're a player or a fan, our app ensures you stay engaged and informed, enhancing your overall AirSoccer experience both on and off the blimp pitch.
